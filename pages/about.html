Grinding Gears is creating a unique coffee experience in Vancouver.

By combining two icons of Vancouver, bikes and coffee, we will bring the quality of the city’s best cafes to the parks and streets.


Serving Groundswell coffee, freshly brewed and using a pour-over method, not only will Grinding Gears will be a convenient option for excellent coffee but we are creating opportunities for sustainable, self-directed employment.


It’s no secret that Vancouver is an expensive city and it can be hard to get a foothold with high rent and low wages. The founding members of Grinding Gears have a vision of expanding to multiple bikes, more working members and ultimately a community of shared success.


As a cooperative, our members share democratic control. Through the coffee bike, Grinding Gears will employ a business model with low overhead that gives our members truly livable wages and financial independence.
