Grinding Gears is creating a unique coffee experience in Vancouver. By combining two icons of Vancouver, bikes and coffee, we will bring the quality of the city’s best cafes to the parks and streets.


Serving Groundswell coffee, freshly brewed using a pour-over method, not only will Grinding Gears will be a convenient option for excellent coffee but we are creating opportunities for self directed and sustainable self employment.


It’s no secret that Vancouver is an expensive city and it can be very difficult to get a foot-hold here. The 3 founding members of Grinding Gears have a vision of expanding to multiple bikes, more worker-members and ultimately a community of shared success.


As a cooperative, we are a democratically controlled organization whose main goal is the financial independence and quality of life of our members and through the coffee bike, Grinding Gears has developed a low-overhead business model that means livable wages and a flexible schedule.


So if you like coffee and you want to know when we’ll be hitting the streets, or if you are interested in joining the co-op as a worker member, please get in touch here.
